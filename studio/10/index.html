Tom Haugomat

2001 a Space Odyssey

Tom Haugomat (1985) is a French illustrator currently based in Paris, where he attended the Gobelins school of visual arts. He uses mainly gouache and Adobe Photoshop for his work, and describes his style as minimalist with a limited color palette, often in the cool pastels. The shapes are smooth and simple, and his use of light is very prevalent and strong. These stylistic decisions come from his desire not to overwhelm or suffocate his users. The images have few details, often leaving faces blank and making backgrounds overblown, like "an overexposed photograph". Most of his illustrations are based on memories and influences from books. He has worked with multiple partners, including Evian, Target, Dots & Co., Le Monde, and Air France.
